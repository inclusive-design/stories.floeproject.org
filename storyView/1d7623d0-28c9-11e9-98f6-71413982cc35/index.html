From Fear to Letting Go

My travels take me to many places that become part of who I am and what I aspire for in the future. Different connections with people who open up their lives and homes to me and all contribute to my sense of identity. Sometimes I reach a point of transformation and it requires a great sense of trust in the flow of life.
